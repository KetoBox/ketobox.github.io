What is a Ketogenic diet?

A Ketogenic diet is a high fat, moderate protein and negligible carbs diet that places the body in a state called Ketosis which uses fats instead of carbohydrates as the energy source and accelerates weight loss among other things. Keto diets have been used to treat epilepsy in medical science for a long time. Practicing a Keto diet in India is notoriously difficult as most of our food is made up of carbs unlike the US or Europe. We consume less meat, cheese and other fatty substances. We at KetoBox provide all the necesssary tools, ingredients and instructions to help you succeed at Keto.
